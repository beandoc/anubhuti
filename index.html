Endoscopic Ultrasound (EUS)

Endoscopic ultrasound diagnostic test (EUS test) is a combination of endoscopy and ultrasound. It is a sophisticated procedure performed for clinical assessment of the digestive tract by producing detailed images of the complete digestive tract, chest, and other organs.

At Anubhuti, we utilize high-resolution EUS to provide unparalleled clarity for preventative screening and complex diagnostic evaluations.
